## ğŸ® Using the Mod

Once built and installed:

1. **Toggle Autoclickers:**
   - Press `R` for left-click autoclicker (or change in Controls)
   - Press `T` for right-click autoclicker (or change in Controls)

2. **Set CPS:**
   - `/lcps 15` - Set left-click to 15 CPS
   - `/rcps 10` - Set right-click to 10 CPS
   - `/lcps` - View current left CPS
   - `/rcps` - View current right CPS

3. **Customize Keybinds:**
   - Open Minecraft Controls menu
   - Find "AutoClicker" category
   - Click and press new key
